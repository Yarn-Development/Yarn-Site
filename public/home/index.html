Yarn Development

A Development team striving to provide unique open source projects to simplify the life of a user.

Created in March 2021, Yarn Development has evolved from an indie Discord Bot team to an organization dedicated to ease the life of the everyday programmer, and in future we aspire to bring the same ease to non-programmers likewise.

How do we assist programmers?

As developers ourselves, we know how much effort and time it takes to do specific tasks and just wish there was a way to simplify the whole process. That's where we come in. In Yarn Development, we compile all of the strenous tasks, and leave only the relevant parts for you to access, saving time, saving effort, but most importantly, leaving time for the real code.

How do we assist non-programmers?

We are currently working on a project called Statisfy, which is a simple to use, yet powerful, statistics API handler. This API handler will allow you to easily access statistics from your favourite social media websites, and display them in a simple, interactive user interface, hence revolutionizing the availabilities of the average social media user.

What are our future plans?

In future, as a team we look to continue to make projects which can assist both programmers and regular users together, but in different aspects, however we may also experiment with mobile game development, and part of our team has been working on an RPG Medieval Game known as Tavern, which is looking to release in 2023. Before releasing as an app we are going to begin ideas as a Discord Bot, and evolve into an app later on. Beta Testers will be needed around the Christmas period, so stay tuned in our Discord server for more updates closer to the time.
